About Zombie Carnival

Zombie Carnival is a mobile lane-defense strategy game where players defend their base from waves of carnival zombies using cannons, blocks, boosts, and smart placement.

Players place cannons across multiple lanes to stop zombies before they reach the base. Each cannon has its own role, attack speed, damage, ammo, and range. Some cannons are better for single-lane defense, while others can hit multiple lanes or deal heavy damage to stronger enemies.

The game also includes a color-matching system. Matching cannon colors deal full damage to matching zombies, while unmatched attacks deal reduced damage. This adds an extra layer of strategy and makes placement decisions more important.

As players progress, they earn coins, unlock upgrades, discover new zombie types, and face more challenging levels. Boosts can help in difficult situations, but winning depends mainly on good timing, smart placement, and choosing the right cannon for the right lane.

Zombie Carnival is designed to be simple to understand, visually fun, and progressively more strategic as players advance through the game.
